NARUTO UZUMAKI

Who is Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is the 9 tails jinchuriki. The 9 tailed beast was sealed inside him by his father Minato Namikaze.

His mother, Kushina Uzumaki was the previous 9 tails jinchuriki.
